# Social Media Manager

## Position Details
- **Department:** Growth & Marketing
- **Reports To:** CMO / VP of Growth
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Manages Number Pii's social media presence across all platforms. Creates engaging content, grows the audience, and drives engagement that supports brand awareness and lead generation.

## Core Skills
- Social media content creation and scheduling (@social-content, @content-creator)
- Platform-specific strategy (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, YouTube) (@linkedin-automation, @twitter-automation, @instagram-automation)
- Social analytics and reporting (@analytics-tracking, @analytics-product)
- Community engagement (@internal-comms-community, @discord-automation)
- Trend identification and newsjacking (@competitive-landscape, @social-content)
- Visual content creation (@brand-guidelines, @figma-automation)

## Technical Skills
- Social scheduling tools (Buffer, Hootsuite, Sprout Social) (@social-content)
- Canva, basic Figma (visual content) (@figma-automation)
- Video editing (basic: CapCut, iMovie) (@social-content)
- Social analytics dashboards (@analytics-tracking, @google-analytics-automation)
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator (supporting BDR team) (@linkedin-automation)
