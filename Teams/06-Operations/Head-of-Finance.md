# Head of Finance

## Position Details
- **Department:** Operations
- **Reports To:** COO
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
ACA/ACCA qualified chartered accountant with experience in tech company finance. Manages financial planning, R&D tax credits, international billing, payroll, and financial reporting.

- Financial planning and analysis (FP&A) (@startup-financial-modeling, @risk-metrics-calculation)
- Management and statutory accounting (@startup-financial-modeling, @cost-optimization)
- R&D tax credits and grant applications (@startup-business-analyst-financial-projections)
- Payroll and benefits administration (@bamboohr-automation, @billing-automation)
- Cash flow management (@startup-financial-modeling, @risk-manager)
- Investor reporting and due diligence support (@startup-metrics-framework, @data-storytelling)
- Tax compliance (@security-compliance-compliance-check, @risk-manager)
