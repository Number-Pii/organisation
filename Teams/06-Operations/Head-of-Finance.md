# Head of Finance

## Position Details
- **Department:** Operations
- **Reports To:** COO
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
ACA/ACCA qualified chartered accountant with experience in tech company finance. Manages financial planning, R&D tax credits, international billing, payroll, and financial reporting.

## Core Skills
- Financial planning and analysis (FP&A)
- Management and statutory accounting
- R&D tax credits and grant applications
- Payroll and benefits administration
- Cash flow management
- Investor reporting and due diligence support
- Tax compliance

## Technical Skills
- Accounting software (Xero, QuickBooks, Sage)
- Financial modelling (Excel/Google Sheets — advanced)
- Payroll systems
- Banking and payment platforms (Stripe, GoCardless)
- Expense management (Pleo, Expensify)

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Financial Planning | Owner | Approve |
| R&D Tax Credits | Owner | Execute |
| Investor Reporting | Contributor | Execute |
| Budget Reviews | Lead | Advise |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Expense claims, routine payments, financial reports
- **Needs approval from:** COO (budget changes), CEO (major financial commitments)
