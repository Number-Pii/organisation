# Social Media Manager

## Position Details
- **Department:** Growth & Marketing
- **Reports To:** CMO / VP of Growth
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Manages Number Pii's social media presence across all platforms. Creates engaging content, grows the audience, and drives engagement that supports brand awareness and lead generation.

## Core Skills
- Social media content creation and scheduling
- Platform-specific strategy (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, YouTube)
- Social analytics and reporting
- Community engagement
- Trend identification and newsjacking
- Visual content creation

## Technical Skills
- Social scheduling tools (Buffer, Hootsuite, Sprout Social)
- Canva, basic Figma (visual content)
- Video editing (basic: CapCut, iMovie)
- Social analytics dashboards
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator (supporting BDR team)

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Social Media Presence | Owner | Execute |
| Product Launches (Social) | Social channel lead | Execute |
| Content Distribution | Distributor | Execute |
| Social Reporting | Owner | Execute |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Social content calendar, post scheduling
- **Needs approval from:** CMO (major announcements), Community & Brand Manager (brand compliance)
