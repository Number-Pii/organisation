# CPO (Chief Product Officer)

## Position Details
- **Department:** Executive Leadership / Product & Design
- **Reports To:** CEO
- **Direct Reports:** Senior PM (Thirty X), PM (Future Products), Lead Product Designer, Product Analyst
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
The CPO owns the product strategy, roadmap, and design direction for all Number Pii products. They have taken a SaaS product from 0→1 and scaled it. They bridge engineering capability with market opportunity, ensuring every feature shipped solves a real problem for sole traders, startups, and SMEs.

## Core Skills
- Product strategy and roadmap planning
- User research and customer discovery
- Data-driven decision making
- Design thinking and UX leadership
- Go-to-market planning
- Stakeholder communication and prioritisation
- Competitive analysis
- SaaS metrics (MRR, churn, NPS, activation)

## Technical Skills
- Product analytics tools (Mixpanel, Amplitude, GA4)
- Prototyping tools (Figma, Framer)
- A/B testing frameworks
- SQL for product analysis
- Agile/Scrum ceremonies and backlog management

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Thirty X Roadmap | Owner | Approve |
| New Product Launch | Lead | Approve |
| Feature Prioritisation | Owner | Approve |
| Design System Changes | Approver | Approve |
| Client Project Design | Advisor | Advise |
| Marketing (Product Positioning) | Collaborator | Advise |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Product roadmap, feature scope, design direction, product hires, product experiments
- **Needs approval from:** CEO (new product initiatives, major pivots), CTO (architectural implications)
