# Principal Consultant — Technology Strategy

## Position Details
- **Department:** Sales & Consultancy
- **Reports To:** Head of Consultancy
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Provides CTO-level guidance for startups and SMEs. Conducts architecture reviews, tech stack selection, and scaling roadmaps. A recognised expert in technology strategy — not a generalist.

## Core Skills
- Technology strategy and advisory (@software-architecture, @senior-architect)
- Architecture reviews and recommendations (@architect-review, @architecture-decision-records)
- Tech stack evaluation and selection (@startup-analyst, @competitive-landscape)
- Scaling roadmaps and technical planning (@cloud-architect, @writing-plans)
- CTO coaching for non-technical founders (@business-analyst, @startup-analyst)
- Technical due diligence (for investors or acquirers) (@senior-architect, @startup-business-analyst-market-opportunity)
- Technical hiring advisory (@interview-coach, @hr-pro)

## Technical Skills
- Modern web/mobile architecture (React, Next.js, Node.js, etc.) (@react-best-practices, @nextjs-best-practices, @backend-dev-guidelines)

- Database architecture (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis) (@database-design, @postgresql)
