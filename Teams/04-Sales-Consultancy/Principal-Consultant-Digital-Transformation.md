# Principal Consultant — Digital Transformation

## Position Details
- **Department:** Sales & Consultancy
- **Reports To:** Head of Consultancy
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
A recognised expert in helping businesses modernise operations, migrate legacy systems, and adopt digital-first strategies. Delivers CTO-level guidance to SMEs undergoing transformation.

## Core Skills
- Digital transformation strategy and roadmapping
- Legacy system assessment and modernisation planning
- Cloud migration strategy (on-prem to cloud)
- Business process re-engineering
- Change management and stakeholder alignment
- Technology vendor evaluation
- Workshop facilitation

## Technical Skills
- Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP — strategic level)
- Enterprise architecture frameworks (TOGAF awareness)
- Integration patterns (APIs, ETL, event-driven)
- SaaS and low-code platform evaluation
- Data migration planning

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Digital Transformation Engagements | Lead consultant | Execute |
| Client Assessments | Advisor | Execute |
| Strategy Workshops | Facilitator | Execute |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Assessment findings, strategy recommendations
- **Needs approval from:** Head of Consultancy (engagement deliverables), VP Sales (pricing)
