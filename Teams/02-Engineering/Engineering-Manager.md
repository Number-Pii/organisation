# Engineering Manager

## Position Details
- **Department:** Engineering
- **Reports To:** CTO
- **Direct Reports:** None (coordinates across all engineering sub-teams)
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
The Engineering Manager owns the processes, hiring pipeline, and coordination that keep the engineering org running smoothly. They manage sprint rituals, engineering culture, onboarding, and career development. They are the operational layer that frees VPs and leads to focus on delivery.

## Core Skills
- Engineering process management (Agile, Scrum, Kanban)
- Hiring and interview process design
- Team health and morale management
- Cross-team coordination and dependency management
- Career development and performance reviews
- Onboarding and documentation
- Engineering metrics and reporting

## Technical Skills
- Jira, Linear, or equivalent project management tools
- Git workflows and branching strategies
- Basic understanding of the tech stack (enough to unblock, not to code)
- CI/CD pipeline awareness

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Sprint Coordination | Facilitator | Execute |
| Engineering Hiring | Pipeline owner | Execute |
| Process Improvements | Owner | Approve |
| Onboarding New Engineers | Owner | Execute |
| Engineering Reporting | Owner | Execute |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Sprint assignments, process changes, PTO requests, onboarding plans
- **Needs approval from:** CTO (headcount changes, role definitions, budget)
