# Product Manager — Future Products

## Position Details
- **Department:** Product & Design
- **Reports To:** CPO
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Leads product discovery and development for Number Pii's future SaaS products. Conducts market research, validates ideas, and takes new products from concept to MVP. Works alongside engineering and design to build new offerings for SMEs.

## Core Skills
- Product discovery and idea validation
- Market research and competitive analysis
- MVP scoping and rapid prototyping
- Customer interviews and problem validation
- Business case development
- Cross-functional coordination

## Technical Skills
- Product analytics tools
- Prototyping tools (Figma, Framer)
- Survey and feedback tools (Typeform, Hotjar)
- Project management (Linear, Jira)
- Basic SQL for data analysis

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| New Product Discovery | Lead | Approve (scope) |
| New Product MVP | Product owner | Approve (features) |
| Market Research | Owner | Execute |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Discovery scope, MVP feature list, user research plans
- **Needs approval from:** CPO (product launch decisions, budget), CEO (new product initiatives)
