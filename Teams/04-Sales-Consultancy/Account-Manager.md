# Account Manager

## Position Details
- **Department:** Sales & Consultancy
- **Reports To:** VP of Sales & Consultancy
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Manages ongoing client relationships, ensuring satisfaction, retention, and upselling opportunities. A former delivery professional who understands engineering, not just relationship management.

## Core Skills
- Client relationship management
- Account growth and upselling
- Project status communication
- Escalation management
- Contract renewal and extension
- Cross-team coordination (Engineering, Design, Consultancy)

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Active Client Projects | Client liaison | Advise |
| Contract Renewals | Owner | Execute |
| Client Escalations | First responder | Execute |
| Upselling | Identifier / Initiator | Execute |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Status reports, meeting schedules, minor scope clarifications
- **Needs approval from:** VP Sales (contract changes, pricing), VP Client Eng (technical scope changes)
