# CMO / VP of Growth

## Position Details
- **Department:** Executive Leadership / Growth & Marketing
- **Reports To:** CEO
- **Direct Reports:** Head of Content & SEO, Community & Brand Manager, Performance Marketing Manager, Social Media Manager
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
The CMO owns all demand generation — SEO, content, paid, social, email, and partnerships. They have a demonstrable track record of growing a SaaS or tech product's audience from zero. Data-driven and creative in equal measure, they shape the narrative AND read the numbers.

## Core Skills
- Growth strategy and demand generation
- Brand strategy and positioning
- Content marketing and thought leadership
- Performance marketing (PPC, retargeting, programmatic)
- SEO strategy (technical and content)
- Community building and social media strategy
- Marketing analytics and attribution
- Budget management and ROI optimisation

## Technical Skills
- Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager
- SEO tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush, Screaming Frog)
- Ad platforms (Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Meta Ads)
- Email platforms (ConvertKit, Mailchimp, Brevo)
- CRM and marketing automation
- A/B testing tools (Optimizely, VWO)

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Marketing Campaigns | Owner | Approve |
| Product Launches (Marketing) | Lead | Approve |
| Content Strategy | Owner | Approve |
| Brand Positioning | Owner | Approve |
| Website SEO | Strategic direction | Approve |
| Hiring (Marketing) | Final interviewer | Approve |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Campaign strategy, content calendar, brand guidelines, marketing hires, tool selection
- **Needs approval from:** CEO (budget allocation, major brand changes)
