# Performance Marketing Manager

## Position Details
- **Department:** Growth & Marketing
- **Reports To:** CMO / VP of Growth
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Runs paid acquisition channels. Manages Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and Meta Ads with a focus on CPA, ROAS, and lead quality. Data-driven approach to scaling profitable campaigns.

## Core Skills
- PPC campaign management
- Conversion rate optimisation
- A/B testing (ads and landing pages)
- Budget management and ROAS tracking
- Audience segmentation and retargeting
- Attribution modelling

## Technical Skills
- Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Meta Ads
- Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager
- A/B testing tools (Optimizely, VWO, Google Optimize)
- Landing page builders (Unbounce, Webflow)
- UTM tracking and attribution
- CRM integration for lead tracking

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Paid Campaigns | Owner | Approve (within budget) |
| Landing Page CRO | Lead | Execute |
| Campaign Reporting | Owner | Execute |
| Product Launches (Paid) | Paid channel lead | Execute |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Ad spend (within budget), campaign creative, targeting changes
- **Needs approval from:** CMO (budget increases, new channels)
