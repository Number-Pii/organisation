# Principal Consultant — Technology Strategy

## Position Details
- **Department:** Sales & Consultancy
- **Reports To:** Head of Consultancy
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Provides CTO-level guidance for startups and SMEs. Conducts architecture reviews, tech stack selection, and scaling roadmaps. A recognised expert in technology strategy — not a generalist.

## Core Skills
- Technology strategy and advisory
- Architecture reviews and recommendations
- Tech stack evaluation and selection
- Scaling roadmaps and technical planning
- CTO coaching for non-technical founders
- Technical due diligence (for investors or acquirers)
- Technical hiring advisory

## Technical Skills
- Modern web/mobile architecture (React, Next.js, Node.js, etc.)
- Cloud architecture (AWS, multi-region, scalability)
- Database architecture (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis)
- Microservices vs monolith trade-offs
- CI/CD and DevOps best practices
- Security architecture (at advisory level)
- API design and integration patterns

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Technology Strategy Engagements | Lead consultant | Execute |
| Architecture Reviews | Lead | Execute |
| CTO Advisory | Advisor | Execute |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Technology recommendations, architecture review findings
- **Needs approval from:** Head of Consultancy (deliverables), CTO (when recommending Number Pii's own technologies)
