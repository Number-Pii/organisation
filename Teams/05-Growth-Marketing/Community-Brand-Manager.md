# Community & Brand Manager

## Position Details
- **Department:** Growth & Marketing
- **Reports To:** CMO / VP of Growth
- **Direct Reports:** None
- **Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Owns the brand voice and community building. Manages Number Pii's presence across community channels, builds engagement, and ensures brand consistency across all touchpoints.

## Core Skills
- Brand voice and identity management
- Community building and engagement
- Event coordination and sponsorships
- Brand guidelines enforcement
- PR and media relations support
- User community management

## Technical Skills
- Community platforms (Discord, Slack communities)
- Brand asset management tools
- Social listening tools (Brandwatch, Mention)
- Design tools (Canva, basic Figma)
- Email marketing platforms

## Project Involvement
| Project Type | Role in Project | Authority Level |
|---|---|---|
| Brand Campaigns | Owner | Execute |
| Community Engagement | Owner | Approve |
| Event/Sponsorship | Coordinator | Execute |
| Brand Guidelines | Enforcer | Advise |

## Approval Authority
- **Can approve:** Community content, event participation, brand asset usage
- **Needs approval from:** CMO (budget, major brand decisions)
