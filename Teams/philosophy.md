## Organisational Philosophy

Number Pii is a **product company that also offers services and consultancy**. This shapes every structural decision:

- **Products First** — Engineering for Thirty X and future products is ring-fenced. Client work never cannibalises product development.
- **Elite Services** — Senior engineers deliver client projects at a premium level, backed by dedicated leadership.
- **Consultancy is Expert-Led** — Principal consultants with recognised domain expertise, not generalist advisors.
- **Security by Design** — Dedicated information security protects our products, our clients' data, and our reputation.
- **No Filler Roles** — Every seat is held by someone world-class. We don't hire to fill boxes.

## Structural Principles

| Principle | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Top 1% Only** | Every hire is evaluated against the absolute best in their field |
| **Products Ring-Fenced** | Product engineering team is dedicated — client work has its own team |
| **Security is Non-Negotiable** | Dedicated InfoSec team reviews every product and client delivery |
| **Seniority Over Volume** | One Staff Engineer > two mid-level. One Principal Designer > three juniors |
| **Remote-First, Global Talent** | Geography is not a constraint — the top 1% are hired wherever they are |
| **Pay at 90th Percentile** | Elite people demand elite compensation |
| **Clear Escalation Paths** | Every role knows who to escalate to and what they can approve independently |

## Hiring Standards

> *"We interview for what they have done, not what they say they can do."*

Every candidate must demonstrate:
- **Shipped work** — real products, real impact, real results
- **Craft evaluation** — a task or presentation revealing true skill level
- **Culture fit** — alignment with Number Pii's ownership mentality
- **Reference checks** — from people who have seen them perform at their best
